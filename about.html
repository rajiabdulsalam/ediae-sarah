My Story

My name is Ediae Sarah, and I am a 200-level Health Information Management student with a passion for the intersection of healthcare delivery and information technology. Growing up in Delta State, I watched healthcare workers struggle with paper-based records and lost patient files — and I promised myself I would be part of the solution.

Health Information Management isn't just a course for me — it's a calling. I believe that well-managed health data saves lives, improves clinical decisions, and strengthens entire health systems. I am building the skills to make that happen.
